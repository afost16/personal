Hi, I'm Alex!

I care deeply about making things nice for the people who need it, not just for design sake. Currently, I'm a junior majoring in Informatics at Indiana University with a cognate in Human Computer Interaction and Design, with minors in Human Centered Computing and Web Design and Development.

In the lab at Proactive Health Informatics, a PhD student, a professor, and I are analyzing the dynamics between care givers and care recipients with dementia from co-design sessions, with the hopes of developing strategies about how to conduct future co-designs sessions involving the care giver and care recipient dyad. I also am immersed in everything creative at TEDxIndianaUniversity. In addition to my own coursework, I'm an undergraduate instructor in the informatics' math foundations course.

Outside of my school responsibilities, I find joy in rock climbing, playing soccer, and passionately supporting my favorite team, Manchester United. Additionally, I enjoy playing video games, primarily Overwatch 2, and exploring various music genres. Thanks for stopping by!
